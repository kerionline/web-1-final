About

Keri Kelly is a freelance designer and a design student at Eastern Washington University. In her freetime she enjoys volunteering with community organizations and her local church, as well as playing the harp, dancing and riding her bike.

As a child, Keri spent most of her time using Microsoft Powerpoint to photoshop pictures of her grandmother into Europe. Nowadays she has upgraded her technology and expanded the things she photoshops into Europe.

Keri has a positive personality which helps her in creating designs or hiking Mount Rainier. She always loves a good challenge. On her off days however, you might see her wandering around the block staring at the neighbor’s flowers.
